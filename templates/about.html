About Us



Golden State Towing, LLC moved from Pleasanton, CA to Sacramento, CA in July 2005. We have been established
since 1998 as a towing and ERS vendor with all of the auto clubs, excluding AAA in the Pleasanton,
Livermore and Dublin area. In May 2005 we signed a contract with AAA to provide service for the “Arden
Arcade” area of Sacramento, thus began the journey to Sacramento. We needed to find a new location, ,
purchase new trucks and get them painted yellow (from our familiar burgundy) and hire quality drivers. ,
This has all been an exciting and rewarding challenge for both Millie and I. My wife Lisa, son Cody and I
sold our home in Livermore and moved to Carmichael in August 2005. Millie commutes from her home in Castro
Valley two days a week.




We look forward to a long successful relationship with you and our other accounts in the wonderful
Sacramento Valley.
